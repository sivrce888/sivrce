How to Buy, Sell, or Rent Property

Prices, demand, and new projects on the real estate market are constantly shifting. That's why having accurate, up-to-date information is so important. Buying or selling property isn't just about how something looks, size, infrastructure, and legal status matter just as much as price and location.

With commercial spaces, the approach is even more specific and differs significantly from searching for a home. Here, location directly affects foot traffic and customer numbers, while the intended use influences licensing requirements and operating costs. Using the filters on our platform when searching for office or warehouse space will save you a considerable amount of time.




For those planning construction or thinking about a long-term investment, land plots are available for sale on Myhome. The platform lets you browse plots across a range of designations - agricultural, residential, and industrial. Every listing helps you see the full picture: whether utilities have been connected and what the access road situation looks like.




Types of Transactions: From Buying and Selling to Daily Rentals




Georgia's real estate market goes well beyond standard purchase transactions. The platform covers all the main types of deals:

Buying and selling: long-term investment and purchasing your own home.
Renting and leasing: both monthly and daily rentals, including in high-traffic tourist areas.
Pledging: using your property to access financial resources while retaining ownership.
Long-term lease: particularly in demand in the commercial sector, especially for office spaces needed over an extended period.

This variety has real practical value. A user can sell an apartment, search for an office space, and check pledging terms all at the same time - without switching between different platforms.




Property Types




Whether you're looking for a small studio or a large-scale property suitable for a hotel, our platform has a wide selection of listings to explore:

Apartments - older and newly built residential buildings, studios, shell-finish and white-frame units
Private houses and country homes - for those who prioritize peace and having their own yard
Cottages - mainly in resort areas like Bakuriani and Gonio
Land plots - various categories and terrain types
Commercial properties - shops, restaurants, and production spaces
Hotels - both operating businesses and properties under construction

This list keeps growing, which means you're always likely to find what you're looking for with us.




Finding the Right Property with the Right Filters




Time is one of the most valuable resources we have. Rather than scrolling through thousands of listings, Myhome offers flexible filters that let you narrow down to only the properties that match your requirements in just a few seconds.

You can filter by:




Price range - so your search stays within your budget
Size and number of rooms - from minimum to maximum
Condition - shell finish, white frame, renovated, or move-in ready
Additional details - balcony, parking space, yard area, and floor number




This level of precision eliminates the disappointment that so often comes from visiting properties in person only to find they don't match the listing.




Finding a Property in the Right Location




Location determines both the price and the quality of day-to-day life. Myhome's map feature and detailed search let you find apartments close to a metro station - a key consideration for getting around Tbilisi.




Infrastructure gets particular attention, too. For parents, being close to a school or kindergarten is often non-negotiable. Students look for locations near their university, while businesses prioritize proximity to main roads. Our filters let you specify particular neighborhoods, streets, or metro stations so you find exactly what you need.

Frequently Asked Question
Can I search for commercial properties on Myhome?

Yes. The platform has a dedicated section for commercial real estate, where you'll find everything from small retail units to large-scale industrial spaces. The filter lets you specify the intended use, office, warehouse, retail, and more, making it easy to find the right fit for your business.




How do I sell a plot of land?

To list a land plot for sale, simply register on Myhome and post your listing. For the best results, upload high-quality photos and provide accurate details, including the cadastral code and land designation - agricultural or non-agricultural. Describe the utilities available on site, such as water, electricity, gas, and internet, as these are often decisive factors for buyers.




How do I find a reliable real estate agency on Myhome?

If you need professional assistance, the platform has a dedicated section bringing together experienced and trusted agents. You can browse their portfolios, view their current listings, and contact them directly. A full list of agencies and their contact details is available in the Agencies section.
