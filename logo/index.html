Concept

One space.
One path. One point.

სივრცე means space. The mark is a single space of brand blue holding an infinite white path — the S — and one orange point: the exact home inside that space. Technology in the geometry, warmth in the point.

The Space

The blue squircle is the space itself — სივრცე. r = 14/48, the exact corner language of every card, tile and module in the product. The logo is literally built from the UI.

The Infinite S

Two tangent elliptical arcs, 250° each, drawn with one continuous stroke — an infinity symbol standing upright. Perfect 180° rotational symmetry around the exact center of the space.

The Space Point

One orange point, bottom-right — the home you were looking for. Its diameter equals the S stroke width: the point and the path are the same gesture, arrived.

Construction

Engineered, not drawn.

Every coordinate is a decision. Nothing is eyeballed — the mark reproduces identically in SVG, CSS and code.
